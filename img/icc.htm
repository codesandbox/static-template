Media release 05 Apr 18

Requests for proposals to manage LED signage programme at the ICC Cricket World Cup 2019

The deadline for submission of proposals is 17 April 2018.

The IBC and the central organising committee for the ICC Cricket World Cup 2019 are requesting proposals from companies interested in providing the LED Signage Production and Management Services for the event to be staged in England and Wales from 30 May to 14 July 2019.

The successful company will be granted the rights and responsibilities to manage the entire LED signage programme, including perimeter signage and behind the wicket (sightscreen) advertising including delivery, installation, production, maintenance, removal and alterations to ensure delivery of a world-class standard appropriate to the tournament.

A proven track record in supplying LED perimeter and sightscreen signage at world-class sporting events is essential as well as sufficient resources and experience to provide all the necessary services, including installation and operation as well as appropriate infrastructure and adequately trained personnel.

The full request for proposals with an in-depth description of the required services and levels of experience for the LED signage production and management programme is available here.

Proposals should be submitted to ICC Brand Licensing Officer Robert Melman (Robert.melman@icc-cricket.com).

The deadline for submission of proposals is 17 April 2018.
