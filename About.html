About Me

             Greetings!!! I'm Jillela Vamshi Krishna, a computer science student hailing from Warangal, Telangana. Currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the prestigious National Institute of Technology Patna, Bihar, I have maintained a commendable CGPA of 8.32 until the 5th semester. My passion lies in full-stack development, decentralized applications, and the fascinating world of augmented reality.

As an avid competitive programmer, I have attained the Expert rating on Codeforces and hold a 5-star rating on Codechef. I thrive on the challenges posed by real-life problems that demand critical thinking, constantly seeking to learn and adopt cutting-edge technologies.

With expertise in web development using the MERN stack, encompassing MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js, I strive to create dynamic, responsive, and user-friendly applications. By harnessing the power of these technologies, I aim to develop innovative solutions that positively impact users.

In addition to my passion for full-stack development, I am deeply intrigued by Web3 and decentralized applications. The potential of blockchain technology to create secure, transparent, and decentralized solutions fascinates me. Continuously expanding my knowledge in this field, I aspire to contribute to the advancement of decentralized technologies.

In my leisure time, I find solace in playing badminton, watching movies, and listening to soul-stirring music. These hobbies provide me with relaxation, inspiration, and a creative outlet to maintain a balanced lifestyle.
