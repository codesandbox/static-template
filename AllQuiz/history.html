  1. Harihara Raya I who ruled the Vijaynagara Empire for the period 1336-1356 belonged to which dynasty?
    • Sangama Dynasty
    • Saluva Dynasty
    • Tuluva Dynasty
    • Aravidu Dynasty
    Ans : Sangama Dynasty
  2. During the Gupta Empire, the term “UPARIKARA” was used for
    • An extra tax levied on all subjects.
    • Periodic supplies of fruits, firewood, flowers etc.
    • was a voluntary offering by people to the king.
    • King’s customary share of the production normally amounting to 1/6th of the production.
    Ans : An extra tax levied on all subjects.
  3. Tuluva Narasa who ruled the Vijaynagara Empire for the period 1491-1503 belonged to which dynasty?
    • Sangama Dynasty
    • Saluva Dynasty
    • Tuluva Dynasty
    • Aravidu Dynasty
    Ans : Tuluva Dynasty
  4. Chola Empire was divided into:

    • Mandalams, Nadu, Kurram & Malkhand
    Ans : Mandalams, Nadu, Kurram & Valanadu
  5. For which period did Harihara Raya II of Sangma dynasty ruled the Vijaynagara Empire?
