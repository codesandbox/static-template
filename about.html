My name is Jerome Biggins

I've worked in the Information Technology field for 10+ years. I discovered my passion for technology at a young age when my mother bought our first VCR. She was having trouble trying to get it to work and didn't understand the features of it. So I played with it until I understood how it worked then showed her how to do it. She pointed out to me how good I was with this and the rest is history. I take great passion in helping others by solving their technical problems which is why I wanted to become a front-end web developer.

While working as a IT technician, I would always get asked if I was able to build websites. I've always been fascinated with the web and always wanted to learn how it works, so one time, I took a course in PHP and when I saw how it helps build robust web pages, I became excited and wanted to learn more. After completing a few projects for people, I discovered that this is how I will serve others. Please let me know how I can solve your problem today.
