Ingredients of Mom's Turbo Dinner Rolls

  1. Prepare 13 oz of (tap) Hot water.
  2. Prepare 1 1/4 tsp of instant yeast.
  3. It's 1 tbs of Brown sugar (or white).
  4. Prepare 3 1/2 of bread flour.
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of salt.
  6. It's 1 tbs of sourdough starter or Discard + 2 tbs flour (optional).

Mom's Turbo Dinner Rolls instructions

  1. Add water sugar and yeast to a large mixing bowl. Whisk thoroughly. Let bloom 3 mins for instant, 10 for active dry yeast..
  2. Optional Step - Add sourdough starter once mixture is cooled and mix well. Add 2 tbs additional flour to total flour..
  3. Add dry ingredients until well incorporated. Cover with plastic Proof 1 ½ hours in warm place, or in oven with light on..
  4. Turn out dough onto floured surface, coat with flour, shape into a large ball. Cut in half, then quarters, then eights = 8 Large rolls, cut each 8th in half for 16 Medium rolls, or cut each 8ths in 3s for 24 small rolls. Shape each piece into a ball on greased baking sheet. Cover with a tea towel, proof for 30 mins..
  5. Preheat oven to 450 while rolls are proofing..
  6. Optional Steps - Egg wash with 1 eggs yolk and 1 tbs water whisked brush the tops of the rolls. *OR* Brush melted butter on before baking. *OR* Garlic Butter. Score rolls in the center..
  7. Place separately on a cookie sheet for crispy rolls, place closer in a 13x9 for pull apart rolls. Bake @ 450 for 12-18 mins depending size and on how dark you want them..
  8. Optional Steps - Brush with melted butter or garlic butter before serving..
