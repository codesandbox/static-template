WELCOME TO FAREED'S

FAREED'S is a Middle Eastern restaurant in Tampa, FL, offering the best selection of authentic Iraqi food. We strive always to provide our customers with mouthwatering and appetizing dishes. Packed with flavor and cooked with the utmost care and dedication to authenticity, you are sure to love everything about our cuisine.

Some of our dishes include:

  • Kebabs
  • Dolma
  • Masgouf Fish
  • Vegetarian Food
  • Chicken Curry and Biryani
  • Shrimp Curry and Biryani
  • Mutton Curry and Biryani
  • Vegetable Curry and Biryani

We only use fresh ingredients, so you can rest assured that we will serve only quality meals. With our professional chefs handcrafting our various Halal dishes, you know that you are getting the best in quality and flavor. Our Arabic restaurant provides a friendly and prompt service for a pleasant dining experience.

We are committed to providing ethnic flavors that are delicious and enjoyable for everyone. We would love the opportunity to show you the love and hard work we put into our cooking, so please visit us at FAREED'S today!
