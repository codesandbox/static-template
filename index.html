Frequently Asked Questions

  • What Is A Digital Agency?

    A digital agency is a company that leverages digital channels to grow their clients’ brands online.

    “Digital agency” is typically used as an umbrella term for a variety of agencies that combine expertise in different channels and technologies such as web design, digital marketing, creative design and app development, for instance.

  • How does our Web Design Service work?

    Once you have discussed with our representative the current situation of your business and the objectives that your new website should meet, you will be able to choose the service plan that fits your needs.

    At this point you will only need to provide us with a few details for us to develop the informative content for the new site.

    For example, consider the following: company name, logo, brand colors, target audience and your products and services.

  • Is it possible to provide feedback on the web design as it is being created?

    Of course, as the project progresses you can provide feedback so that you have a website that meets your expectations and that you are proud to showcase on the Internet.

  • Why should I choose you to create my website?

    Having a custom built website (not templates) by the our team gives you peace of mind, as it includes a state-of-the-art, professionally designed website with an accelerated turnaround time compared to other providers.

    Typically, it takes one to two weeks to complete a website, but we will work with you to understand your program and your requirements.
