Johannes Kepler (1571-1630)

Johannes Kepler: Famous German Astronomer

Johannes Kepler

Important Dates In Kepler's Life

  • Dec. 27, 1571 – Johannes Kepler was born.
  • 1584 – Started to attend his Lutheran School.
  • Sep. 17, 1589 – Started to attend the University of Tübingen.
  • 1597 – marries Barbara Mueller.
  • 1600 – Begins work with Tycho Brahe, most productive years of his life, was assigned to study the orbit of Mars.
  • 1601 – Death of Tycho Brahe. Kepler continues to with Brahe's data until 1612.
  • 1609 – Published the book New Astronomy, which includes Kepler's first and second laws of planetary motion.
  • 1609-1611 – Kepler gave support to some of Galileo’s discoveries made by a telescope.
  • 1619 – Published World Harmony, which included Kepler's third law of planetary motion.
  • Nov. 15, 1930 – Kepler's Life comes to an end.
