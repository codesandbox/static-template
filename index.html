Mindful of Mental Health

Girl Scout Gold Award



About Me


My name is Alina Zaman. I’m currently a senior in high school and I have been a Girl Scout for 13 years. I decided to complete my Gold Award during my junior year. I wanted to reach a large audience of teenagers and young adults who may or may not have someone they can reach out to. Mental Health is a very important thing and many times is ignored due to other critical aspects of our lives. However, we should always take that moment to care for ourselves and learn about how to cope with the stress of our everyday lives. I decided to complete my Gold Award for the sole reason of helping those who are unsure of how to help themselves.

About My Project


My project aims to target the issue of mental health in teens, which often goes highly undiscussed. It has always been an ongoing issue and I know many of you might not be comfortable opening up to anyone. However, you shouldn’t have to deal with everything alone. Whether it’s you or a friend or just someone you know who is going through a hard time, everyone should be educated on ways to cope with these issues. This is a safe place for you to learn more about how you feel and how to control your emotions and work on coping and healing.
