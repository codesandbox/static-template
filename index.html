Hi there!

My name is Peppin, Pepping Hambocks. I'm the owner of this tavern: The Drunken Orc. Hopefully you aren't intimidated by my appearance. I may be an orc, but I am a good man.

How my tavern started as a business

My tavern started as a humble shack where I served my family small dishes I worked on in my free time. A gnome, however, invited me to work in his restaurant for a while because he was impressed by my talent. I left his restaurant after a while, because I didn't feel happy working there and being eyed at for being an orc. So I opened my own humble tavern.
