Penne with pesto genovese
  • Penne pasta
  • Pesto genovese
  • Hard cheese grated
  • Olive oil
  1. Boil the pasta for 8 minutes.
  2. Add a handful of olive oil and mix in the pesto.
  3. Then sprinkle with grated hard cheese (Parmigiano or Grana Padano) and serve.
