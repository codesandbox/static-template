Custom Order

Planning a beach wedding or special event?

Island Importer specializes in comfortable, made-to-order, natural fiber clothing for destination weddings. If we do not stock the size or color that you wish to order, we will gladly custom-make the garments for you and/or your entire wedding party - including children! And for all children, our $20 custom-order fee is waived!

With only three simple steps, you'll have your wedding attire custom-made according to your size and color specifications!

  • From the product page, select the ‘Custom Order’ tab.
  • Select from our range of colors. Note: As colors may vary per monitor, we strongly suggest ordering swatches before placing custom orders. Order swatches
  • If ordering a standard size, or a modification to a standard size, enter your size in the 'Standard size' field. Enter your body measurements in the appropriate fields. Click on the help icons next to each field for how-to photos and instructions.
  • Enter your wedding/departure date, agree to terms, then proceed to checkout.

Regular delivery on custom orders takes 4 - 6 weeks.

Expedited delivery on custom orders takes 1 - 3 weeks.

Custom orders requiring expedited delivery will be subject to additional fees of $25 for the first item, and $7 for each additional item. This fee includes DHL Express Intl. delivery directly from our factory to your door. Expedited delivery of suits is $35 per suit.

Note: Custom orders are non-refundable unless garment is flawed and/or not made according to the measurements you provided. In such cases, we will gladly remake your order. Custom orders may not be canceled or refunded 24 hours after order placement.

Essential measurements for:

Jackets:

  • Chest : Place the measuring tape high under your arms at the widest point, covering your chest and your shoulder blades. The tape should be completely level. As you breathe normally, have a friend insert a finger between your chest and the measuring tape. This suit measurement yields both your chest and suit size.
  • Stomach : Measured around the widest section of your stomach to ensure a proper fit of your jacket and/or vest.
  • Overarm : To take this measurement place the tape measure over the outside of the arms across the thickest part of the chest.
  • Jacket length : This suit measurement determines the ideal length from the bottom of the collar to the bottom of the jacket. Measure from where your neck meets your back to a point just below the curve of your buttocks.
  • Sleeve length : In this suit measurement, determine your sleeve length by measuring from the outer edge of your shoulder to a point that just covers the wrist.

Dresses:

  • You may custom order our stretch jersey dresses in standard sizes: S(0-4), M(6-8), L(10-12), XL(14-16).
  • For silk dresses, please provide the following measurements:
    • Cup Size
    • Bust circumference
    • Waist circumference
    • High Hip circumference
    • Low Hip circumference
    • Cross Shoulder length
    • Clavicle Center to Waist length
    • Center Back of Neck to Waist length
    • Center Back of Neck to Low Hip length
    • Shoulder to Bust Point length
  • Dress Length (Measure from waist down to where you want the dress to fall in front)

Shirts:

  • Neck circumference
  • Chest circumference
  • Cross Shoulder length
  • Sleeve Length (If long-sleeve - measured from the outer edge of the shoulder to the first knuckle of the thumb).
  • Desired Shirt Length (Not required). If a non-standard shirt length is desired, measure from base of neck, down to at least your low hip. Too long is always better than too short!

Pants:

  • Waist circumference
  • Low Hip circumference
  • Inseam (canmeasure existing pant)
  • Thigh circumference (at widest part)
