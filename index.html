What squad is right for you?

It's important to find your playstyle first in order to find what type of squad would best suit you

Finding your playstyle is easy. First think about what type of player you are. Do you like to be more agressive or more passive, do you like long range or short range.

Once you figure this out you can then search for players to add to your squad or you can look for an already built squad to join.
