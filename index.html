Learn how to tie a tie in less than 5 minutes

1st step

Start with the wide end of your tie on the right, extending about 12 inches below the narrow end on the left.

2nd Step

Wrap the wide end over the narrow end

3rd Step

Pull the end up through the loop

Last Step

Hold the knot with your finger and pull the tie closer to your neck.
