WELCOME


Our mission is simple. Hildreth Dental enhances the health and well-being of our patients with exceptional care, compassionate people, and a welcoming environment. As Kennesaw dentists, we're proud to make a positive impact on our community by:

  • Helping patients be their healthiest self
  • Fixing dental concerns and alleviating pain
  • Treating others the way we'd like to be treated

At our practice, you always come first.

Enjoy Your Time at the Dentist

We’ve designed a patient experience that stands out from other Kennesaw dentists. Here you’ll find top-rated service, a team who remembers your name, and empathetic support when you’re feeling anxious. Don’t worry about brushing up on oral health trivia or deciphering treatment costs — we’ll clearly explain everything so you know exactly what to expect.
