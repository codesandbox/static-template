Chocolaty Date-Nut Balls

Brazil nuts boost thyroid function by 85%

A great snack anytime!

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups pitted dates
  • 2 Tbs. chocolate chips
  • 1/3 cup coarsely chopped Brazil nuts
  • 2 Tbs. rolled oats
  • 2 Tbs peanut butter
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Pinch salt

Steps

  1. In food processor, pulse date and chocolate chips until coarsely chopped
  2. Add remaining ingredients.
  3. Process until sticky ball forms
  4. 2 Using moist fingers
  5. Roll about 2 Tbs. mixture into ball
  6. Repeat to make about 8 balls total
  7. Chill until set, about 20 min
Serves 4
