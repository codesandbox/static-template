Sweet Potato Pudding

Description

Have for Thanksgiving Meal

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lbs. medium sweet potatoes (about 3)
  • 1/2 cup butter, at room temp
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 Tbs. self-rising cornmeal
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup pecan halves
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Line baking sheet with foil.
  3. fierce potatoes.
  4. Bake on baking sheet until tender, 1 hr. Cool.
  5. Reduce oven temp, to 325 Degrees.
  6. Coat 8" square baking dish with cooking spray.
  7. Peel potatoes; place in mixing bowl.
  8. Add butter; mash until smooth.
  9. On medium speed, beat in eggs 1 at a time.
  10. Add granulated sugar, milk, cornmeal, 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract and salt;
  11. beat until smooth.
  12. Pour mixture into baking dish.
  13. Arrange pecans on top
  14. Sprinkle brown sugar, then remaining 1/2 tsp. vanilla over top.
  15. Bake until center is set and cooked through, about 45 min.
