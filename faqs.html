Frequently Asked Questions

   How long will it take my books to arrive?

We drop off orders at the United States Post Office on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays. Once the Post Office has your order it generally takes between 2 and 10 days for your package to be delivered.

   What are your shipping costs?

We mail our packages at the United States Post Office so that we can take advantage of their Media Mail rates. Rates start at $3.00 and slowly increase from there.

   Can I get a refund if I'm unhappy with my purchase?

We accept return items for a full refund as long as they are in good condition.

   How is your shop environmentally friendly?

All of our packaging is eco-friendly and recyclable.
