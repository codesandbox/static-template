Pokémon TV is showing a collection of episodes featuring love.

12 February 2021

Right Now Pokémon TV is showing a collection of classic episodes of Pokémon the Series featuring love.

"Friendship is in the air this February when Pokémon follow their hearts in curated episodes on Pokémon TV."

Just when you thought Pokémon couldn't possibly get any cuter, they go and do something adorable like fall in love. Whether it's Ash's Butterfree falling head over wings for a wild pink Butterfree, or a Quilladin trying to woo an Eevee with a heart-shaped Vine Whip, we've assembled some of the most memorable Pokémon interactions from Pokémon the Series on Pokémon TV. There's even an exciting marriage proposal between Pokémon Trainers, but whether it's as cute as watching an Ultra Beast befriend Pikachu is highly questionable.
