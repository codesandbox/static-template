Upcycle Utopia

Upclycling is a method of renewing old products which would have been thrown away to landfill. Instead, it is given a new "life" by transformations or reburbishment to be reused again and again. Upcycling represents a variety of processes by which “old” products get to be modified and get a second life as they’re turned into a “new” product.

As people are now moving towards a minimalist approach where "less is more", we will aim to insprire you with simple hacks to upclycle produts easily found at home. Sustainable thinking and upcycling is increasing in the fashion industry as well as building furniture from plastics and manufacturing.

Feel free to support any businesses that match your values and get inspired to buy less and recreate unique pieces from what you can readily find at your home.
