Origins

Baseball is a game where players of the defending team try to out the player who is batting. Both the teams take turns at batting and fielding. Three consecutive outs from each team make an innings, and nine innings make a game. The objective of each team is to win by scoring more runs and try to prevent opponents from scoring runs.

The first game of baseball ever played took place in June 1846 in Hoboken, New Jersey. In 1869, and the first ever team in professional baseball was the Cincinnati Red Stockings.
