sarang info

When the journey of Sarang began, we thought of growing and selling of Indoor and outdoor plants, organic vegetables.Each day towards this goal of growing plants, making this place self sustainable and useful to many people in the village, we came across many village level problems and hurdles for the growth of village people economically, technologically,creatively.We aimed that SARANG should be the birthplace for most of the solutions that can be solved with more innovation,technology and co operation from villagers

Feture Aim

To create awareness about waste management among people in the village and collect all the segregated waste generated. We Recycle and Upcycle all the segregated waste and try to generate income out of it. All the profits will be used again for the development of village. In this whole journey we want to create more employment to villagers.
